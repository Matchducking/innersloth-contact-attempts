# Dataset Notice

## Status: DISCONTINUED

This dataset has been permanently removed and will not be updated or republished.

## Reason for Removal

Out of an abundance of caution and to ensure full compliance with international data protection laws and child safety regulations, including but not limited to:

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
- Various international child protection frameworks

All data previously collected has been deleted. We recognize that responsible data handling, especially concerning platforms used by minors, requires strict adherence to privacy and safety standards.

## Reporting Online Safety Concerns

If you encounter concerning behavior online, particularly involving minors, please report it to the appropriate authorities:

### International Hotlines
- **INHOPE**: International network of hotlines for reporting illegal content online
  - Website: https://www.inhope.org/
  - Find your country's hotline: https://www.inhope.org/EN/members

### United States
- **National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)**
  - CyberTipline: https://www.cybertipline.org/ or call 1-800-843-5678

### United Kingdom
- **Internet Watch Foundation (IWF)**
  - Website: https://www.iwf.org.uk/
  - Report: https://report.iwf.org.uk/en

### European Union
- **EU Safe Internet**
  - Find your country's hotline: https://www.betterinternetforkids.eu/web/portal/practice/helplines

### Australia
- **eSafety Commissioner**
  - Website: https://www.esafety.gov.au/
  - Report: https://www.esafety.gov.au/report

### Canada
- **Canadian Centre for Child Protection**
  - Cybertip.ca: https://www.cybertip.ca/

### Platform-Specific Reporting
- Always report concerning behavior directly to the platform where it occurred
- Most gaming platforms and social networks have built-in reporting tools

### Law Enforcement
- For immediate threats or emergencies, contact your local law enforcement
- In the US: FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3): https://www.ic3.gov/

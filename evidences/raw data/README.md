## Overview

This directory contains **unaltered raw datasets** collected during investigations into systemic abuse on the platform.
The files here exist to preserve **primary evidence** exactly as observed, without interpretation, filtering, or narrative framing.

This data is the foundation upon which all analyses, reports, and disclosures are built.

## Contents

The raw data in this folder may include JSON dumps containing:

* **In‑game names** used by predator accounts
* **User IDs** as exposed by the game or related services
* **Friend codes** used to initiate or maintain contact with minors
* **Chat messages**, preserved verbatim, demonstrating patterns of behavior, grooming attempts, or coordination
* **Account linkages**, where observable, across multiple identifiers

No attempt has been made to normalize, sanitize, or “clean” this data beyond what is required for storage integrity.

## Why This Data Is Preserved Raw

Raw data is preserved because:

* It allows **independent verification** of claims
* It prevents accusations of cherry-picking or manipulation
* It exposes **scale, repetition, and systemic failure**, not isolated incidents
* It documents behavior patterns that cannot be dismissed as anecdotal

Any summarized statistics or conclusions elsewhere in this repository are derived directly from these files.

## Important Notes on Interpretation

* Presence in this dataset indicates **documented suspicious or abusive behavior**, not a legal conviction.
* Identifiers are recorded **as they appeared at the time of collection**; usernames and friend codes may change.
* Chat logs are included to demonstrate **behavioral patterns**, not for shock value.

If this data is uncomfortable to read, that discomfort reflects the reality it documents.

## Relationship to Inaction

All data in this folder was collected **after or alongside repeated attempts to report these issues** through official and public channels.

The existence of this folder is not accidental.
It exists because **institutional responses failed**, forcing community-level documentation to fill the gap.
